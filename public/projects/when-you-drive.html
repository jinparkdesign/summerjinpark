Park Your Phone

Graphic & Motion Design | 2020

This is a motion design project done for my college project at Seneca College. The topic of my project is the one of the most important rule when driving, which is 'DON'T USE YOUR PHONE!' I created it with Illustrator and Aftereffects. Before making the final version, I created storyboards, colour pallete and moodboards.
