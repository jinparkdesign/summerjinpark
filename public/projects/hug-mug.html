HUG MUG Brand Book Design

Branding & Layout | 2020

HUG MUG is a coffee brand created for a school project. In this branding project, I created a brand name, concept map, moodboard, logo and brand book of coffee product.

CONCEPT MAP

Before I started branding HUG MUG, I created a concept map and came up with few keywords of abstract user scenarios and specific to product related to coffee. This process let me figure out what people can feel and think about coffee and helped me to go to the next step which is creating a moodboard and logo.

MOOD BOARD

This is a moodboard that I created for this branding project. The 11 keywords that I chose were 'cosy', 'calm', 'peaceful', 'relaxing', 'quiet' ,'friendly', 'comfy', 'restful', 'warmth', 'homelike', and 'aroma'.

LOGO BRAIN STORMING

The first step was brain storming. I sketched several designs. I wanted to convey some warm image in my logo. In the beggining, I started to draw some ‘hugging a mug of coffee’ images because the keywords of my logo is ‘Hug’ and ‘Mug’. However, my first sketches looked a bit obvious to me. So, I started to sketch some heart shape, cute coffee bean and a cat in the logo. Then, I wanted try some simple logos as well while still conveying a hugging image. I tried to express the human shape as a line, and in the other thumbnail, I simplified the hand shape. After this, I tried to sketch my logo only with the letters as well. I made the letter U as a coffee cup.

HUG MUG LOGO FINAL

This is the final logo for HUG MUG. It is simple but still convey the image of embracing the coffee mug. This logo gives warmth & peaceful vibe.
